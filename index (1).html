Our Story

Built on one big idea

Genium was founded in 2021 with a simple but radical belief: humanoid robots should make human life richer, not replace the human experience. We set out to build machines that feel like partners, not products.

Today, with three robot platforms and a growing global team, we are turning that belief into reality — one interaction at a time.

Enhancing human connection, not replacing it.

🤖

Genium Headquarters · Est. 2021

What We Stand For

Our values

Every decision at Genium flows from the same set of core beliefs.

01
Human First

We design every feature around how people actually live and work — not what's technically impressive.

02
Radical Safety

No feature ships if it compromises the safety of the people around our robots. Full stop.

03
Earned Trust

Transparency in how our AI works, what data it uses, and what it never will — always available to everyone.

04
Long-Term Thinking

We build for the next 50 years, not the next funding round. Durability and repairability are core design principles.
