Who We Are

About Genium

At Genium, we believe the future of artificial intelligence is personal.

We develop intelligent humanoid companions designed to work alongside people in everyday life. By combining advanced robotics, adaptive AI, and human-centered design, we create technology that can assist with learning, creativity, productivity, and personal growth.

Our goal is simple: build companions that enhance human capability without replacing human connection.

Genium companions are designed to understand individual preferences, adapt to changing needs, and provide meaningful support over time.

Our Mission

Built for People.

To create intelligent humanoid companions that enhance human capability, confidence, and quality of life through adaptive partnership.

Robotics Engineering Genium Headquarters
Mechanical Engineer
Salary Range: $70,000 – $110,000
About the Role

Help design and develop the physical systems that power Genium companions. Mechanical Engineers work on mobility, structural design, articulation, and durability to create humanoid robots capable of operating naturally in the real world.

Key Responsibilities
  • Design robotic structures and movement systems
  • Develop and test prototypes
  • Collaborate with AI and software teams
  • Improve performance, reliability, and repairability
Qualifications
  • Degree in Mechanical Engineering, Robotics, or related field
  • Experience with CAD software and prototype development
  • Strong problem-solving and analytical skills
  • Ability to work in a collaborative team environment
Product Experience Genium Headquarters
Chief Experience Officer (CEXO)
Salary Range: $160,000 – $240,000
About the Role

Lead the vision for how users interact with Genium companions. The Chief Experience Officer ensures every product delivers intuitive, personalized, and meaningful experiences that align with our mission of human-centered technology.

Key Responsibilities
  • Oversee user experience strategy
  • Guide personalization and accessibility initiatives
  • Coordinate across engineering, AI, design, and support teams
  • Shape the future of human-companion interaction
Qualifications
  • Master's degree in a relevant field
  • Leadership experience in technology or product development
  • Strong communication and strategic planning skills
  • Passion for human-centered innovation
